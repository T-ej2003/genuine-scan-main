# DPA Handoff Brief

This handoff brief is for legal review and completion. It reflects the current implementation and should not be treated as final legal language.

## MSCQR implementation facts relevant to a DPA

- MSCQR is a hosted SaaS platform with role-based access for super admins, licensee admins, manufacturers, and public verification users.
- MSCQR processes operator account data, verification metadata, support evidence, and incident records.
- MSCQR stores and accesses authentication cookies, verification session cookies, and browser-side state.
- MSCQR may process customer email addresses and verification-session context.
- MSCQR may use AWS-hosted infrastructure and object storage for application and evidence handling.
- MSCQR may use Sentry for observability when configured.
